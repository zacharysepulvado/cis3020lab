Booking Process

  1. Submit the booking form with your session type, preferred date, and location idea.
  2. I review your inquiry and follow up to confirm availability and session details.
  3. A minimum 50% deposit is required to reserve the date. Clients may pay more than 50% or pay in full at the time of booking.
  4. After the session, I send a preview gallery so you can select favorites.
  5. The remaining balance is due before final edited gallery delivery.
  6. Final edited images are delivered through a private online gallery.

Current Availability

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session?

Submit a booking inquiry with your session type, preferred date, and location idea. I will follow up to confirm availability and finalize the details.

How much is the deposit?

A minimum 50% deposit is required to reserve a session date. Clients may also choose to pay more than 50% or pay in full at the time of booking.

When is the remaining balance due?

The remaining balance is due before final gallery delivery.

How long does it take to receive the photos?

Most sessions have a turnaround time of 2 weeks. Event photography has a turnaround time of 3 weeks.

How are photos delivered?

Final edited photos are delivered through a private online gallery with full-resolution download access.

Do you travel?

Yes. Travel is available depending on the location. Additional travel fees may apply outside of the local area.

Can I choose my favorite photos?

Yes. After the session, I send a preview gallery where clients can like and select favorite images before final editing.

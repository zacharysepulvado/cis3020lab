Booking Process

  1. Submit a booking inquiry with your session type, preferred date, and location idea.
  2. I confirm availability and discuss the session details with you.
  3. A minimum 50% deposit is required to reserve the date.
  4. After the session, I send a preview gallery so you can select favorites.
  5. Final edited images are delivered through an online gallery.

Deposit Policy

A minimum 50% deposit is required to reserve a session date. Clients may choose to pay more than 50% or pay in full at the time of booking. Remaining balances are due before final gallery delivery.

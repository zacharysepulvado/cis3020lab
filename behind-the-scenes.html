What Happens Before the Session

  1. Client reaches out with session type, preferred date/time, and location ideas.
  2. I confirm availability and suggest locations if needed.
  3. We agree on a plan (time, place, style, and goals for the shoot).
  4. I prepare a simple shot list and any gear needed for the session.

During the Session

  • I guide posing and help the client feel comfortable. Ask them questions and get them talking about themselves. People love talking about themselves.
  • I capture a mix of posed and candid photos.
  • I adjust lighting and camera settings based on the scene.
  • I review a few shots on the camera with the client to make sure we're getting what the client wants.

Editing and Delivery

  1. I select the best images from the session and send a preview gallery with favorites-only access, watermarked and without download enabled.
  2. I edit for exposure first, then color, remove blemishes & distractions, and ensure consistency across the set.
  3. I export high-quality images for download and web sharing.
  4. I deliver the final photos through an online gallery using Pixieset.

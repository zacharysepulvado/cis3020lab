This website is the foundation for my photography booking project. In the future, it will showcase my work and provide a streamlined booking experience. For now, it explains the booking process, with future plans to add back-end features like availability calendar automation and deposits/payments.

Booking Process

  1. Client reaches out with session type, preferred date/time, and location of interest if applicable.
  2. I confirm availability and location details.
  3. We lock in the session and I send confirmation details.
  4. Future upgrade: online booking + deposit + automated reminders.
